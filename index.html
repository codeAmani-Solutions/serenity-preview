Why T&T Serenity Care?

Founded by nurses, driven by a passion for quality of life.

The Leader's Vision

Teresia Kirumba’s journey in nursing is defined by leadership in the moments that matter most. As a Deputy Incident Commander during the COVID-19 pandemic, she was responsible for the safety and operational success of over 300 frontline staff. Her expertise in disaster management and health administration isn’t just about logistics; it’s about creating systems of safety and reliability that allow true, compassionate care to flourish, ensuring every detail is managed so that individuals receive the unwavering support they deserve.

The Healer's Touch

Teresia Kimani’s path is one of deep, personal connection and hands-on care. With over 13 years at the bedside, she has dedicated her career to understanding the nuanced needs of every person she serves. Her work during the pandemic involved not only clinical skill but profound empathy, easing the fears of vulnerable patients. It is this heart for service and her gift for building trust that ensures every resident at T&T Serenity Care is seen, heard, and honored as an individual.

Who We Serve

Providing dedicated, person-centered support for adults with developmental disabilities across Central Florida under the APD iBudget Waiver.

Individuals & Families

We partner with individuals seeking a safe, empowering group home environment and families in need of reliable, high-quality respite and personal support services.

Support Coordinators

We are a trusted, responsive partner for Waiver Support Coordinators, ensuring clear communication, timely documentation, and exceptional care for your clients.

Community Partners

We collaborate with healthcare providers, pharmacies, and APD representatives to create a seamless network of support that prioritizes the health and well-being of every resident.

Join Our Team

Make a difference in a supportive, professional environment. We're looking for compassionate individuals to join our mission.

Direct Support Professional (DSP)
Full-time Part-time

Support adults with developmental disabilities in a licensed group home. Deliver person-centered daily living support, community inclusion, documentation and coordination.

Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)
Full-time Part-time

Provide personal care, observation and documentation in a home-like setting. Assist with ambulation, hygiene, nutrition and activities; collaborate with RNs and leadership.

LPN / RN
Part-time PRN

Provide clinical oversight, care planning and coordination with providers; coach staff on documentation and protocols; medication oversight per APD and nursing standards.
